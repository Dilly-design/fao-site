FAQ

Before you message us.

How much lead time do you need?+

We typically need at least 48 hours' notice, and can often accommodate urgent requests depending on availability.

Do you serve outside Riyadh?+

We cover all of Riyadh, and can discuss coverage in other cities for standing contracts and larger events.

Is there a minimum guest count?+

We serve everything from a 5-person meeting to 500+ guest events — pricing scales with size and service type.

How does invoicing work for companies?+

We issue an official tax invoice, and can arrange recurring billing for monthly contracts or ongoing employee meal programs.
